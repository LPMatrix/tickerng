# Marketing Ideas — TickerNG (Scored & Prioritized)

**Context** (assumed from product and LAUNCH_STRATEGY):

| | |
|---|---|
| **Product** | TickerNG — NGX stock research (Discovery + Verification), AI reports, free tier, share links. |
| **ICP** | Nigerian retail investors, analysts, researchers; people who want fast, structured NGX research. |
| **Stage** | Early (Alpha → Beta). Landing + signup live; v2 features shipped. |
| **Goal** | Signups and usage; build owned channels (email, blog, app). |
| **Constraints** | Likely solo/small team; cost-sensitive; need speed to signal. |

**Scoring:** MFS = (Impact + Fit + Speed) − (Effort + Cost). Range −7 to +13. Only ideas with **MFS ≥ 1** recommended; max **5** ideas; bias toward **speed to signal** and **low cost**.

---

## 1. Founder-led content (X + LinkedIn)

**MFS:** `+11` (Extremely high leverage — do now)

| Dimension | Score | Note |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Impact | 4 | Builds audience and trust; drives signups and shares. |
| Fit | 5 | NGX/fintech audience is on X and LinkedIn; you’re the builder. |
| Speed | 5 | Post today → impressions and link clicks within days. |
| Effort | 2 | 1–2 posts/week; reuse launch narrative and screenshots. |
| Cost | 1 | Time only. |

* **Why it fits**  
  Your launch strategy already calls for “1–2 social posts” and “Short threads: How I research NGX stocks in 2 minutes.” This is the highest-velocity channel for early stage with no budget.

* **How to start**  
  1. Draft one thread (X): “How I research any NGX stock in under 60 seconds” with 5–7 steps and a demo GIF or link to TickerNG.  
  2. Post the same story in one LinkedIn post (problem → how TickerNG helps → CTA).  
  3. Add a simple calendar reminder: 1–2 value posts per week (NGX tip, screenshot of a report, “what I look for before buying”).

* **Expected outcome**  
  Within 2–4 weeks: measurable link clicks to landing/signup; optional follow-up DMs or replies you can convert to early users.

* **Resources required**  
  Your time; one demo GIF or screenshot; link to signup in bio and in post.

* **Primary risk**  
  Consistency. One post won’t move the needle; 4–6 weeks of steady, value-first content will.

## 2. Email capture on landing + simple nurture

**MFS:** `+10` (Extremely high leverage — do now)

| Dimension | Score | Note |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Impact | 4 | Owned list for launch, feedback, and retention. |
| Fit | 5 | Fintech/investor audience expects email; launch doc already prioritizes it. |
| Speed | 4 | Capture starts immediately; first sequence can go out within a week. |
| Effort | 2 | One form on landing; 3–5 email sequence (e.g. welcome, how to use, one report example). |
| Cost | 1 | Free tier of Resend/SendGrid or similar. |

* **Why it fits**  
  LAUNCH_STRATEGY says “Collect at signup and via ‘Get NGX research tips’ on landing.” You already have signup; adding a non-signup email capture builds a second funnel and a direct channel for “TickerNG exists” and “New feature” announcements.

* **How to start**  
  1. Add an email field + “Get NGX research tips” (or “Notify me when we launch”) above or below the main CTA on the landing page.  
  2. Store emails in DB or a small tool (e.g. Resend + list).  
  3. Write 3 emails: (1) Welcome + what TickerNG does, (2) How to run your first Discovery/Verification, (3) One example “report highlight” or tip + CTA to try.

* **Expected outcome**  
  Within 2 weeks: first batch of emails captured; first sequence sent; open/click and signup-from-email measurable.

* **Resources required**  
  Front-end form; backend or 3rd-party for storage + send; copy for 3 emails.

* **Primary risk**  
  Low volume at first. Mitigate by driving traffic from X/LinkedIn (idea #1) and from one SEO post (idea #4).

## 3. Product Hunt launch

**MFS:** `+9` (Strong opportunity — prioritize)

| Dimension | Score | Note |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Impact | 3 | Spike in traffic and signups; social proof and backlinks. |
| Fit | 4 | SaaS/tool; global PH audience plus Nigerian investors who browse PH. |
| Speed | 5 | One day of focus; results within 24–48 hours. |
| Effort | 2 | One strong listing (tagline, gallery, first comment); coordinate with 1–2 supporters. |
| Cost | 1 | No cash; time only. |

* **Why it fits**  
  Launch strategy already mentions “Product Hunt (if desired)” at full launch. Doing it earlier gives a clear “launch moment,” a shareable link, and a chance to test positioning with a broad audience.

* **How to start**  
  1. Pick a Tuesday–Thursday; create the PH listing (tagline: e.g. “Full NGX stock research in under 60 seconds”).  
  2. Prepare 3–5 screenshots or a short video (Discovery → shortlist → Verify → report).  
  3. Line up 2–3 people to upvote and comment in the first 2–3 hours.  
  4. On launch day: post the link on X and LinkedIn; ask your email list (once you have one) to support.

* **Expected outcome**  
  One-day traffic spike; 10–50+ signups if the listing resonates; a permanent PH page for future “we were on Product Hunt” credibility.

* **Resources required**  
  PH account; assets (screenshots/video); 1–2 hours on launch day; small network to seed votes/comments.

* **Primary risk**  
  PH can feel “one-day.” Use it as an event to collect emails and followers, not as the only channel.

## 4. One comparison / alternative page (SEO)

**MFS:** `+8` (Strong opportunity — prioritize)

| Dimension | Score | Note |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Impact | 4 | Captures “best NGX research tool” and “TickerNG vs” intent. |
| Fit | 5 | Direct fit for consideration-stage investors comparing options. |
| Speed | 3 | Indexing in 1–4 weeks; rankings 4–12 weeks. |
| Effort | 2 | One long-form page; no scaling yet. |
| Cost | 1 | Your time; no paid distribution. |

* **Why it fits**  
  People search “best NGX stock research tool,” “how to research Nigerian stocks,” or “TickerNG alternative.” One dedicated page (e.g. “Best tools to research NGX stocks in 2025” with TickerNG as primary recommendation, or “TickerNG vs manual research”) satisfies intent and gives you a second entry point besides the homepage.

* **How to start**  
  1. Choose one target: e.g. “Best NGX stock research tools” or “How to research Nigerian stocks before buying.”  
  2. Write one 1,200–1,800 word page: criteria (speed, structure, NGX-specific, cost), short comparison table or bullets, and a clear “Try TickerNG” CTA.  
  3. Add the page under `/blog` or `/resources`; link from footer or landing “Resources.”  
  4. Submit URL in sitemap (already have sitemap); optionally share the URL once on X/LinkedIn.

* **Expected outcome**  
  Non-brand impressions and clicks in 4–8 weeks; compounding if you add more comparison or “how to” pages later.

* **Resources required**  
  Copy; one new route; internal link from homepage or nav.

* **Primary risk**  
  Slow first signal. Pair with founder-led content so you’re not dependent on SEO alone in month 1.

## 5. One pillar SEO post (“How to verify an NGX stock”)

**MFS:** `+7` (Strong opportunity — prioritize)

| Dimension | Score | Note |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Impact | 4 | Ranks for “verify NGX stock,” “research Nigerian stocks”; builds E-E-A-T. |
| Fit | 5 | Exactly what TickerNG does; aligns product and content. |
| Speed | 2 | Same as comparison page: 4–12 weeks for meaningful traffic. |
| Effort | 3 | One thorough post (1,500–2,500 words); structure and checklist. |
| Cost | 1 | Time only. |

* **Why it fits**  
  LAUNCH_STRATEGY already suggests: “How to verify an NGX stock before investing,” “NGX ticker symbols and where to look.” This is the classic “pillar” post that attracts high-intent readers and supports the product narrative.

* **How to start**  
  1. Outline: What to verify (financials, valuation, risk, catalysts), where to find data, and how a structured report helps (tie to TickerNG without making it an ad).  
  2. Write and publish under `/blog` or `/resources`; add to sitemap and link from landing (“Learn how we think about NGX research”).  
  3. Reference this post in X/LinkedIn posts when you talk about “how I research NGX stocks.”

* **Expected outcome**  
  Steady non-brand search traffic in 6–12 weeks; stronger positioning as “the place that teaches NGX research” and converts readers to signups.

* **Resources required**  
  Writing time; one new route; internal links.

* **Primary risk**  
  Slow. Treat as compounding asset; pair with ideas #1 and #2 for faster feedback.
