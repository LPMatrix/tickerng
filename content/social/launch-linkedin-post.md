**Body:**

Before TickerNG, my research process was the same every time: open multiple tabs, grab numbers from different sources, and manually build a view on financial health, valuation, and risk. Fine for one or two tickers. Painful when you’re screening a sector or comparing a shortlist.

I wanted one place to get a structured report — the same checklist I’d run myself, but generated consistently. So I built TickerNG.

**What it does:**  
• **Discovery** — Ask in plain English (e.g. “best dividend-paying banks on NGX”) and get a shortlist of stocks that match.  
• **Verification** — Enter any NGX ticker and get a full report: financials, valuation, risk factors, growth catalysts, and a bull/bear verdict.

Reports are structured, not chat. You get sections you can scan, confidence levels where we have them, and shareable links so you can send a read-only report to a colleague or client. No buy/sell signals — just a clear view so you can verify the stock yourself.

It’s live and free to use. If you research Nigerian equities and have feedback, I’d love to hear it — comment or DM.
